ABOUT MYSELF

After graduating high school in 2017, I believed that life would find a spot for me with little work - as I was doing nothing to improve myself. I had no clue what I wanted to do in life. The only thing I did know was that I wanted to help change the world for the better.

After talking about this with my brother, he had mentioned programming. I did not know a single thing about programming at the time, but I wanted to give it a try. I created an account for Code Academy and fell in love with Python. I went to a nearby university for Software Development and that is where I had developed most of my skills for OOP - although the courses and projects were mainly backend development.

Since graduating with an Associates Degree in Software Development, I had moved states and it was pretty difficult to land a job with my skillset, along with the competition. I ended up feeling discouraged and eventually had a few jobs doing unrelated work for a couple of years.

After moving again, I had decided to try my hand at coding again. This time, I had to learn everything from the ground-up by myself, with a focus on the frontend. I made a promise to myself that I will not let this go to waste and to always continue learning.

Nowadays, I study as much as I can, building web applications while learning new skills to add to my new and existing projects. I will say that since relearning everything again, I have improved a lot and I feel like I am ready to tackle on real world projects.
